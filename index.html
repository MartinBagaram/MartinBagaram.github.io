Here you can find a short list of domains in which my competences expand

Forestry and Natural resources

Research operations has a myriad of applications in forestry especially in Precision forestry. Research operation is used for instance to to determine the spatially explicit harvest scheduling models, multiple-criteria forest planning, best thinning sequences, the evaluation of non-wood forest products, appraisal of non tangible forest products. Further examples can be found on the webpage of my advisor Dr Sándor F. Tóth

Agriculture

I can use operation research for applications in farm management. The models are to decide what to grow where, how to rotate crops, how to expand production and where to invest among many others.

Other type of questions are:

    - How to blend together livestock feeds or fertilizer at minimum cost ?
    - How to distribute the farm products in particular milk/eggs and other frequent products ?
    - How to plan and harvest farm products ?

Transportation

I have competences in transportation network problems such as the maximum flow, the minimum cost or the shortest path and the spanning tree. Transportation network has many applications in domains like water transportation, cabling and electricity distribution, road network to minimize the cost and the environmental impact. You may click to add your own node and drag to add an arc. An example of the solution / the shortest path implemented in JAVA is provided in projects and the implementation is available on github

Wildlife

The wildlife field is one of the most interesting. Applications are what are the best landscapes for a demanding wildlife ? How to organize surveys and design parks that are ecologically friendly ? How to design a reserve to promote an extincting species ? etc.
